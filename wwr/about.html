Rafting Momentum

Our purpose at Rafting Momentum is to share our love of exploring the rivers around us and have fun doing it. There are countless rivers from the Mighty Mississippi to the Snake River in Idaho and the Rappahannock river in Virginia. As well as many small ones. We want to explore them all with you.

History

In 2013 a group of three friends decided to take their love of the water and adventure and share it with the world. Starting on the white waters of the Colorado river this trio gave guided tours of their favorite waterways. As interest grew, so did their business. They grew organically, learning the nearby rivers and then expanding nationwide.
